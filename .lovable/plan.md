

# ThreadKnit — Premium Digital Showroom

## Brand Identity & Design Language
- **Color palette**: Deep navy, warm beige/cream, and soft gold accents — reflecting premium craftsmanship
- **Typography**: Elegant serif for headings, clean sans-serif for body — communicating sophistication
- **Motto woven throughout**: *"Softness You Can Wear. Crafted for Comfort. Styled for Life."*
- **Overall feel**: A luxury fashion showroom meets B2B buyer confidence

## Page 1: Homepage — Product Showcase (Entry Page)

### Hero Section
- Full-width cinematic hero with a subtle fabric-texture animation in the background
- Animated tagline reveal: the motto fades in word by word
- A prominent CTA: "Explore Our Collections"

### Product Catalog (Main Content)
Three category sections with immersive motion graphics:

**Ladies' Knitwear** — T-shirts, Tank Tops, Hoodies, Dresses, Nightdresses, Onesies, Trousers
- Product cards with hover effects that reveal fabric details (composition, GSM, print type)
- Smooth scroll-triggered fade-in animations as products enter the viewport

**Men's Collection** — Polo Shirts, T-shirts, Jackets, Sweatshirts, Hoodies, Trousers, Shorts
- Same premium card treatment with technical specs overlay on hover

**Children's Products** — Pajamas & Outfits
- Playful yet professional presentation

Each product card will display: product image, item name, fabric description, GSM, color, and print/effect technique — pulled directly from your catalogs.

### Category Navigation
- Sticky horizontal filter bar to switch between Ladies / Men's / Children's
- Smooth animated transitions between categories
